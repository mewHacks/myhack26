# Sponsors & Strategic Analysis

## 🔵 Google
**Priority: AI Agents & Cloud-Native Solutions**

Google has declared the end of the "AI pilot era." The big push now is the **Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform** — a unified surface for building, securing, running, and governing AI agents. They're also championing no-code agent building so anyone can create trigger-based workflows without writing code.

**What they want to see:**
- Agentic AI (autonomous AI that acts on your behalf)
- Google Cloud / Vertex AI integration
- Real-world problems solved using Gemini APIs
- Enterprise productivity, data workflows, or multi-agent systems

**Bonus points for:** multi-agent orchestration, ADK usage, Cloud Run deployment

---

## 🟠 Cradle Fund
**Priority: Scalable, Investable Malaysian Startups**

Cradle's 2026 focus is "scaling up with precision" — bridging pre-Series A to Series A via **Cradle Elevate**. They back startups with validated solutions that aren't yet ready for larger private rounds.

**Top sectors for 2026:**
- AI & digital services
- Semiconductors
- Fintech
- Healthtech
- Green technology / renewable energy

**What they want to see:**
- Commercially viable, real-problem-solving idea
- Clear path to market (not just a cool demo)
- Strong value proposition for **Malaysia or ASEAN**
- Realistically investable — think startup, not experiment

---

## 🟣 MoneyLion
**Priority: AI-Powered Fintech & Personalized Financial Services**

MoneyLion's core differentiator is AI — e.g., just-in-time underwriting that adjusts credit eligibility daily based on real-time income/spending, replacing 60 human underwriters with zero. After their **US$1B acquisition by Gen Digital**, their white-labeled AI recommendation platform is being scaled globally.

**What they want to see:**
- AI-driven financial wellness
- Embedded finance or credit accessibility
- Personalized financial tools for **underserved consumers**
- Smarter budgeting, lending, or financial literacy powered by AI/ML

---

## 🟢 Devoteam
**Priority: Agentic AI, Cybersecurity & Cloud Transformation**

Tech consulting firm specializing in cloud, cybersecurity, data and AI. Their 2025 TechRadar flagged agentic AI and generative models as dominant trends — over half of their 150 tracked technologies now incorporate AI.

**Six strategic focus areas:** AI · Managed Platforms & Sustainability IT · Cybersecurity · Product Development · Cloud · Data-driven Intelligence

They strongly emphasize **sustainable tech** — greenhouse gas reduction, ethical AI, and embedding ESG into digital transformation.

**What they want to see:**
- Enterprise AI adoption
- Cloud architecture or cybersecurity solutions
- Responsible / green tech
- AI strategy bridged to real business outcomes (not tech for tech's sake)

---

## 🎯 Cross-Sponsor Sweet Spot

To appeal to **all four sponsors at once**, build something that:

| Requirement | Satisfies |
|-------------|-----------|
| Uses AI agents / AI-powered automation | Google + Devoteam |
| Solves a fintech or financial inclusion problem | MoneyLion |
| Commercially viable and scalable in Malaysia/ASEAN | Cradle |
| Responsible, real-world impact | All four |

---

## Strategic Notes

- **Lead tech:** Gemini + Vertex AI + Cloud Run — satisfies Google mandatory requirement and signals enterprise readiness to Devoteam
- **Domain sweet spot:** AI-powered fintech targeting underserved Malaysians hits MoneyLion and Cradle simultaneously
- **Pitch framing:** Frame as a startup with a go-to-market plan, not a hackathon project — Cradle cares about investability
- **SDG angle:** Financial inclusion (SDG 1, 8, 10) + responsible AI (SDG 9, 17) covers the criteria requirement and Devoteam's ESG focus
- **Differentiation risk:** Healthtech is oversaturated in past winners — fintech + agentic AI is underexplored territory
